Media and Society- COMM: 2084

This course is taught by Professor Jen Mackay

The purpose of this course is to look at and consider various forms of media and its impact on our society. This course examines censored books, social media, cultural issues media across the world.

Jen Mackay started her career in broadcat journalism, continuing on she returned to written journalism. She eventually landed at Virginia Tech and took a great interest in social media analytics as well as its effects on our society and culture. I personally greatly enjoy media and society as it allows me to have a deeper understanding of the fascination that I as well as the rest of generation have with the media and social media.
