Course Description

The purpose of this course is to look at and consider various forms of media and its impact on our society as a whole. This course examines censored books, social media, cultural issues and media across the world.


About my Professor

Jen Mackay started her career in broadcast journalism, continuing on she returned to written journalism. She eventually landed at Virginia Tech and took a great interest in social media analytics as well as its effects on our society and culture.


My opinion on the course

I personally greatly enjoy media and society as it allows me to have a deeper understanding of the fascination that I as well as the rest of generation have with the media and social media.
