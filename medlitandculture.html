This course is taught by PhD candidate Brooke Covington

Literature, Medicine, and Culture looks at how literature assits us in the understanding and nature of diseases, illnesses, and suffering across various cultures. The course uses many different mediums to convey this, such as poetry, fiction, movies, memoirs, art, as well as essays. We use the knowledge gained from the many texts and annicdotes that we read to reflect on the connections between literature, medicine, illness and healing.
