Course Description

Literature, Medicine, and Culture examines how literature assits us in the understanding and nature of diseases, illnesses, as well as suffering across various cultures. The course uses many different mediums to convey this, such as poetry, fiction, movies, memoirs, art, as well as essays. Students in the course use the knowledge gained from the many texts and annicdotes to reflect on the connections between literature, medicine, illness, healing and pain.
