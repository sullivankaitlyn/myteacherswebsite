Course Description

PR cases is an analysis of public relations cases, the course explains how students can relate and learn from them. The course examines theories, research techniques, planning methods, various Public Relations strategies, PR standards, as well as ethics in Public Relations.


About my Professor

Neeka Logan began her career in coorporate Public Relations, she then retired from the cooperate world to begin her career as a professor in 2006. Dr. Logan has won various awards for both her research on communication strategies as well as implementation of said strategies.


My opinion on the course
